Service Provided

Consultation

During an Ayurvedic consultation, I engages in a comprehensive conversation with the individual, asking questions about their medical history, lifestyle, diet, daily routines, sleep patterns, and any specific symptoms or concerns they may have. Based on the information gathered, We analyzes the individual's dosha (bio-energetic constitution), assesses the balance or imbalance of the doshas, and identifies any underlying causes of health problems. They then provide personalized recommendations and treatment plans to restore balance and promote overall well-being.

Dietary Recommendations

We suggests specific foods, spices, and herbs to incorporate into the individual's diet based on their dosha and health condition. They may advise on dietary restrictions, meal timings, and food combinations.

Lifestyle Modifications

Recommendations are provided regarding daily routines, sleep patterns, exercise, and stress management techniques to promote a healthy lifestyle aligned with the individual's dosha.

Herbal Remedies

We may prescribe specific Ayurvedic herbal formulations, such as powders, tablets, or oils, to address specific health concerns or to support overall well-being.

Panchakarma or Detoxification

If necessary, then we may suggest Panchakarma therapies, which are Ayurvedic detoxification and rejuvenation procedures aimed at eliminating toxins, balancing the doshas, and restoring health.

Ayurvedic Treatments

We may recommend specific therapies or treatments such as Abhyanga (Ayurvedic oil massage), Shirodhara (pouring medicated oil on the forehead), or Swedana (herbal steam therapy) to address specific health issues.
